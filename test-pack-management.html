Integrated Document Control

Replace Email Chains with Structured Digital Workflows

Managing the flow of engineering documents between client and contractor is one of the most time-consuming and error-prone activities on any large project. HEMG Project Lifecycle Commander™ includes a built-in Document Control module that replaces email chains, shared drives, and paper transmittals with a structured, auditable digital workflow.

Client-to-Contractor Document Sharing

The client uploads approved engineering documents — P&IDs, isometrics, specifications, and standards — directly to the platform. Contractors access only the documents relevant to their scope. No email attachments. No version confusion. Every document is revision-controlled and access-logged. The correct revision is always available — to every authorised party — from any device.

Digital Transmittal Management

Every document issued generates an automatic transmittal record containing: document title, revision number, date issued, recipient, and acknowledgement status. Full audit trail from first issue to final approval. No paper transmittals. No missing signatures.

Contractor-to-Client Document Submission

Contractors upload their deliverables — method statements, inspection test plans, welding procedures, and as-built drawings — directly through the platform for client review. Documents are version-controlled. No lost emails. No missing attachments. No 'which version did you send?' queries.

Client Review & QA/QC Approval Workflow

Clients and QA/QC teams review submitted documents in-platform, add comments, approve, or return for revision. Every action is timestamped and attributed to the reviewer. The approved document library is always current, always complete, and permanently accessible to all authorised parties for the lifetime of the project.
