The story of how Helios Engineering & Mechanical Group (HEMG) redefined industrial execution through disciplined digital control.

Our Mission

"To transform industrial execution into a disciplined, data-driven process that eliminates human error and guarantees mechanical integrity across the asset lifecycle."

Founding Background

HEMG was founded in 2014 by a group of veteran mechanical engineers who witnessed the catastrophic risks of manual, fragmented records in the energy sector. They saw that even the most advanced facilities were being compromised by simple errors during the final assembly of critical joints.

Our first implementation—a digital flange management system for a major refinery—proved that by digitizing the execution step, we could achieve 100% leak-free startups. From that single success, we have expanded into a comprehensive integrity platform used globally.
