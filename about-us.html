Company Story

HEMG — Helios Engineering & Mechanical Group — was built from the ground up by practitioners who spent their careers on the hardest industrial projects in the world: offshore platforms, nuclear facilities, pharmaceutical plants, petrochemical complexes, and refinery turnarounds across South Asia, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.

The founding team carried one shared, recurring frustration across every project they had worked on. The greatest risks were never caused by poor engineering design. They were caused by the gap between what the engineering specification demanded and what actually happened in the field during execution.

!

A torque value not properly verified.

!

A weld record hand-written in a logbook that could not be found during a regulatory audit.

!

A test pack signed off on paper with no photographic evidence.

!

A commissioning team spending weeks manually compiling a handover dossier while the client's project clock ran.

The Proof of Concept

Major Refinery Turnaround, India

The first HEMG implementation was a digital flange management system deployed on a major refinery turnaround in India. The outcome was immediate and measurable: 100% of critical joints verified to engineering specification, zero startup leaks, and a handover package that was auditor-ready within hours rather than weeks.

From that single proof of concept, HEMG has grown into a comprehensive mechanical integrity platform deployed across Oil & Gas, Nuclear, Pharmaceutical, Petrochemical, Power Generation, Offshore FPSO, Water & Desalination, Semiconductor, and Construction environments globally.

Today, HEMG operates from three hubs — India, United Kingdom, and UAE — serving plant owners, EPCs, and contractors across the complete mechanical asset lifecycle.

The Core Objective

Mission Statement

"To transform industrial execution into a disciplined, data-driven process that eliminates human error and guarantees mechanical integrity across the full asset lifecycle."

We believe the gap between an engineering specification and a verified execution record should be zero. Every bolt tightened, every weld made, every system tested — traceable, verifiable, and permanently recorded. We build the tools that close that gap.

The Industry Gap

Founding Background — Why HEMG Was Built

The industrial world has invested hundreds of billions in engineering design, simulation, and planning technology. Yet the execution layer — the physical work of assembling, welding, testing, and commissioning — has remained largely paper-based, manual, and fragmented for decades.

The HEMG founding team had seen these patterns repeated across two decades of field experience. The solution was not more training manuals or better spreadsheets. It was a purpose-built digital execution platform that enforced engineering logic, captured evidence automatically, and made compliance the path of least resistance for every person on site.

Documented Industry Consequences

60%

Over 60% of hydrocarbon leaks originate from flanged joints assembled without verified torque control. (ASME PCC-1 / ESA)

Weld

Weld repair rates of 5–15% are routinely accepted as industry normal, when digital control can achieve under 2%. (AWS Standards)

3-6w

Commissioning delays of 3–6 weeks on large projects driven primarily by documentation inconsistencies. (CII Findings)

Audit

Regulatory audit failures are disproportionately caused by documentation gaps rather than work quality. (Compliance Gap)

The Workforce First

Our Commitment to the Industrial Workforce

HEMG was designed from its first line of code with one non-negotiable principle: the platform must work for the professionals who do the work.

Industrial execution depends on the skill, precision, and expertise of field professionals — the pipe fitters assembling flanges in a refinery, the welding inspectors validating joints on an offshore platform, the commissioning engineers closing out punch lists under intense schedule pressure.

These are highly trained individuals whose expertise is the foundation of every safe and efficient industrial operation. HEMG honours that expertise by providing tools that match their professionalism.
