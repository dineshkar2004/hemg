Industry: Oil & Gas
100% Reduction in leaks

Major Refinery Turnaround, Middle East

Challenge:

Recurring startup delays due to flange leaks on high-pressure steam systems. Over 15% of joints required manual rework after initial pressurization.

Solution:

Implemented Bolted Joint Master™ to digitize the tightening workflow, enforcing engineering-grade torque values and real-time photographic verification.

Outcome:

Achieved a 100% leak-free startup. Saved an estimated $1.2M in avoided delay costs and labor rework.

Industry: Petrochemical
25% Efficiency Increase

Mega-Scale Petrochemical Complex, UAE

Challenge:

Coordinating completion across 12,000+ test packs involved high risk of data fragmentation and missed punch items, compromising safety.
