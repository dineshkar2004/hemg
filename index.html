Core Platform Pillars

Equal focus across bolted joints, welded joints, test pack management and execution learning—independent but digitally connected.

Control

Bolted / Flange Joint Integrity

Digital control and traceability of bolted and flanged joint assembly to prevent leaks, rework, loss of containment, and unplanned shutdowns.

Key Capabilities

  • Torque- and tension-controlled tightening strategies
  • Verification with photo + parameter evidence
  • Traceability of bolts, gaskets, tools, and personnel
  • Controlled re-assembly verification during shutdowns
  • Long-term joint history for integrity reviews
  • Aligned with global flange management and mechanical integrity best practices.
Traceability

Welded Joint Traceability

Universal welded joint traceability for fabrication control, inspection management, regulatory compliance, and long-term mechanical integrity.

Key Capabilities

  • Digital weld registers + mapping linked to drawings
  • Linkage of WPS, PQR, and welder qualifications
  • NDE tracking, repairs, re-inspection, acceptance
  • Metallurgy traceability across steel/alloy systems
  • Weld history supporting maintenance planning
  • Applicable across pressure equipment, piping systems, and structural welds.
