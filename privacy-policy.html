Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 09, 2026

1. Introduction

Helios Engineering & Mechanical Group (HEMG) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tells you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Data We Collect

When you use our contact forms or engage with our platform, we may collect the following information:

  • Identity Data: Full Name, Job Title/Role.
  • Contact Data: Work Email Address, Country.
  • Professional Data: Company Name, Industry.
  • Inquiry Data: Information regarding your specific requirements or interests.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly for professional purposes, including:

  • Responding to your inquiries and providing requested information.
  • Scheduling platform demos and technical discussions.
  • Providing technical support and professional services.
  • Improving our website and service offerings based on your feedback.

Email Usage: Your work email is primarily used for direct communication regarding your inquiry. We do not engage in spam marketing. Any future communications will allow you to opt-out at any time.

4. GDPR & PDPA Compliance

HEMG complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) regarding the collection, use, and retention of personal data. We follow best practices to ensure:

  • Transparency: We are clear about what data we collect and why.
  • Security: We implement robust technical and organizational measures to secure your data.
  • Rights: You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time.

5. Data Security

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way, altered, or disclosed.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at: info@hemgroup.com
